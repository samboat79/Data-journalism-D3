Health Risks by demographics

Analysis

Based on correlation analysis and charting, it is found that income has the most significant impact on obesity. Lower income people tend to be more obese.

Further middle aged people tend to be more obese. There seems to be a co-relation between obesity and smoking, which further increases health risks

A good co-relation was not found for poverty rates. This may be due to cost of living aspects.
